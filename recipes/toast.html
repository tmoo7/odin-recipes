Cruchy toast. Yum!

Everyone loves bread! What if you toasted it?

If you like the flavor of plain bread then try this hack and toast it. Toasted bread can be eaten with unlimited toppings. As many as your heart desires.

Ingredients

  • Bread

Steps

  1. Put bread in toaster and choose your desired toastyness settings.
  2. Eat plain or enjoy with your favorite toppings such as: butter, jam/jelly, cinnamon sugar, etc.
